Introduction

Welcome to Endlos Furnitures ("we", "us", or "our"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting any personal information you provide to us through our website, https://endlos-hospitality.netlify.app ("Site"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your data, and your rights in connection with that information.

By using our Site, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect two types of information:

  1. Personal Information
    When you fill out our contact form or communicate with us, we may collect:
    • Full Name
    • Email Address
    • Phone Number
    • Company Name
    • Any other information you voluntarily provide

We use this information solely to communicate with you, respond to your queries, or provide relevant information about our products and services.

  1. Non - Personal Information
    When you browse our site, we may automatically collect certain technical data:
    • IP address
    • Browser type
    • Device type
    • Operating system
    • Pages Visited
    • Time and date of visit

    This information is collected via cookies and analytics and is used for internal analytics and to improve user experience.

How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries or requests
  • To provide information about our services and products
  • To improve our website functionality and user experience
  • To perform website analytics and understand usage trends
  • To ensure the security and stability of our website

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal data to any third parties.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect and store information. Cookies help us:

  • Recognize repeat visitors
  • Understand how users interact with our website
  • Improve performance and user experience

You may disable cookies via your browser settings, but this may affect certain functionalities on our website.

We use third-party analytics services such as Google Analytics to help us understand website usage. These tools may collect your IP address and certain browsing behaviors on our Site.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

We ensure these third parties are contractually bound to protect your data and only use it for the purpose agreed upon.

Your data is stored securely on our website host BigRock Solutions Ltd and/or associated cloud services. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data, including encryption, firewall protection, and access controls.
