Brand

Wyndham Hotels

Location

Project Overview

The hospitality case goods for a Days Inn hotel in the USA were designed, manufactured, and exported from our in-house manufacturing facility in India, which included metal bed frames, nightstands, drawers, upholstered headboards, work desks, wardrobes, vanities, and lounge seating. All the hospitality furniture for the Days Inn hotel was delivered within project timelines, meeting the durability, quality, and design expectations required by the Days Inn FF&E specification guidelines.
