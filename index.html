Project Management Overview

Our project management process covers everything from initial planning to final installation, including space planning, brand approvals, FF&E manufacturing, and on-time delivery, thus providing end-to-end project execution for custom hotel furniture.

1. Layout & Measurements

We collect accurate site measurements from the client, which we use to design smart, space-efficient furniture layouts, tailored to hotel room requirements, enhancing guest comfort.

2. 3D Visualization and Renders

To help clients visualize their finished hotel rooms before production, we develop realistic 3D visualizations and interior renders for them.

3. Keyline Drawing

We prepare detailed keyline drawings with exact dimensions and specifications to serve as a blueprint for precise furniture manufacturing.

4. Brand Approval and Agreement Signing

Final designs made according to hotel brand standards are submitted for approval to ensure satisfaction. Once approved, agreements are signed to officially begin production.

5. Production

Manufacturing begins under strict quality control systems, following approved design specifications for each item so that the final product meets hospitality grade durability.

6. Dispatch, Delivery and Installation

Each item is securely packaged and dispatched to the project location in the USA followed by on-site installation to ensure seamless and timely handover.
